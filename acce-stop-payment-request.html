Stop Payment Request

If your payee hasn't yet processed your pre-authorized payment or deposited your cheque, you can make a Stop Payment Request online. We'll do our best to cancel the payment, but it may not always be possible.

A fee of $12.50 applies, unless Stop Payment Requests are included in your banking package.

To improve your chances of stopping a payment on time, please provide as much information as possible.

Requests submitted on business days between 4:00 a.m. and 9:30 p.m. (local time) will be processed the same day. Otherwise, we'll process your request on the next business day.

If you're missing required information about the pre-authorized payment or cheque, please visit your local branch or call us at 1-800-769-2511.

Stopping multiple pre-authorized payments or cheques

To stop multiple pre-authorized payments or cheques, you'll need to submit a separate request for each one. Alternatively, you can learn about other ways to stop multiple pre-authorized payments.

You can't use Stop Payment Requests to cancel payments made at a branch, through Online Banking, ATM or Telephone Banking.

In such cases, call us at 1-800-769-2555 before 6:00 p.m. local time on the day of the payment and we'll do our best to stop the transaction.
